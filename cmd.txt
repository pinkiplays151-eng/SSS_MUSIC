.play <song name or url> (alias: .p) - Joins your voice channel and plays the song.
.stop (alias: .s) - Stops the music and leaves the channel.
.skip (alias: .sk) - Skips the currently playing song.
.queue (alias: .q) - Displays the current music queue.
.loop (alias: .l) - Toggles queue looping.
.volume <0-100> (alias: .v, .vol) - Sets the music volume.
.clear (alias: .c) - Clears the queue.
.join (alias: .j) - Makes the bot join your current voice channel.
