Bot Commands

Prefix: $
Music Playback
$play <song/URL>
Aliases: $p
Joins your voice channel and plays the song. Supports YouTube & Spotify links.
$skip
Aliases: $sk
Skips the currently playing song and plays the next one in queue.
$pause
Aliases: $pa
Pauses the currently playing song.
$resume
Aliases: $r, $res
Resumes a paused song.
$stop
Aliases: $s
Stops music playback and disconnects the bot from the voice channel.
$nowplaying
Aliases: $np, $now
Shows the currently playing song with a progress bar.
Queue Management
$queue
Aliases: $q
Displays the current music queue with up to 10 songs.
$clear
Aliases: $c
Clears all songs from the queue.
$loop
Aliases: $l
Toggles loop mode for the current song.
Voice Channel
$join
Aliases: $j
Makes the bot join your current voice channel.
$volume <0-100>
Aliases: $v, $vol
Sets the music volume. Value must be between 0 and 100.
Tips
YouTube & Spotify
Use $play <song name> for YouTube search or paste a full Spotify/YouTube URL directly.
Dashboard Controls
All playback controls are also available in this dashboard — no need to type commands in Discord!
Default Prefix
The default prefix is $. You can change it in config.json under the prefix field.
